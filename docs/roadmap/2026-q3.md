# Q3: Community + Evidence (Jul -- Sep 2026)

The third quarter focuses on scaling the skill library, generating quantitative evidence for the SKILL.md approach, and establishing a contributor community.

## Key Deliverables

### 100 Skills on Hub

- Expansion to **100 verified skills** including foundation model skills:
  - **AlphaFold** -- protein structure prediction
  - **ESM** (Evolutionary Scale Modeling) -- protein language model
  - **scGPT** -- single-cell foundation model
- Skills contributed by both the core team and external authors

### Reproducibility Benchmark

- **20 published analyses** reproduced using ClawBio skills vs. original repositories
- Metrics: time to reproduce, success rate, output fidelity
- Target: >90% success rate in under 5 minutes per analysis
- Published as preprint on bioRxiv

### Agent Error Rate Benchmark

- **100 pharmacogenomics queries** run through SKILL.md-guided agents vs. unconstrained LLMs
- Metrics: factual accuracy, guideline compliance, hallucination rate
- Target: <2% error rate with SKILL.md guidance
- Published as preprint on bioRxiv

### Pilot Research Groups

- **5+ pilot research groups** actively using ClawBio in their workflows
- Joint publications documenting real-world usage
- Feedback loop informing spec and platform improvements

### Community Infrastructure

- **Leaderboards**: top contributors ranked by skill count, downloads, and citations
- **Domain collections**: curated skill sets for pharmacogenomics, single-cell, population genetics
- **Ambassador programme**: recognised contributors with early access and advisory roles
- **Badges**: contributor, reviewer, domain expert

### Advisory Board

- Board constituted with **4+ domain experts** spanning:
  - Clinical genomics
  - Bioinformatics workflows
  - AI/ML in biology
  - Research reproducibility

### Conference Presence

- Workshops and presentations at:
  - **ISMB** (Intelligent Systems for Molecular Biology)
  - **ECCB** (European Conference on Computational Biology)
  - **ASHG** (American Society of Human Genetics)
